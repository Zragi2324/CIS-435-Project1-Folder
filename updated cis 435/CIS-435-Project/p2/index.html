Common pets

There are many types of pets to choose from, and you may not know where to start looking. The best way to know which pet is best for you is to first understand if you have any kinds of allergies that may be triggered because of your new pet. You don't want to get a pet and shortly find out your allergic to them. If you just want to know which pets to start with, here are some common pets to choose from:

  • Cats
  • Dogs
  • fish
  • Aquatic Turtles

Caring for your pet

Caring for your cats

For cats Its important to have high-quality cat food, a great way to determine your cats diet is by having your vet assess you new cat and determine a diet for it. Your cat also requires grooming, brushing and combing your regular is important. Your cat needs to have access to fresh water at all times. Make sure you cat has a soft bed. Liter boxes are a must for indoor cats, you should place the liter box somewhere accessible for the cat. Make sure to clean the liter box regularly. It is also essential that you make sure your cat is up-todate on vaccinations.

Caring for your dog

Providing premium-quality dry food helps with providing a well-balanced diet for your dog. You should restrict your self from providing your dog with human food because this might lead to vitamen and mineral imbalances. Your dogs needs to have access to clean fresh wayer at all times. Dogs require exercise, so a great way to achieve this by taking frequent walks with your dog. Be sure to regularly clean your dog, checking for ticks or fleas. Check with your vet for your dog's required vacinations.

Caring for your fish

Fish require a fish-tank, and the bigger the tank the better. Fish require room to swim, so the extra space helps with this. The positioning of your fish tank is important because depending on the breed of fish you have, heating is essential for their survival. When it comes to feeding your Fish its important to learn the recommended amount of fish food and the times per day you should be feeding your fish before getting one. For a fish, having a clean fish tank is important to their surviaval. Investing in a decent filter and regularly cleaning the fish tank helps with this.

Caring for your turtle

For starters, a good step would be learnign the recommended habitate size for your turtle, a good rule of thumb is 10 gallons per inch of turtle, keep in mind that adult turtles will require more room as they grow. Any uneaten food or feces need to be scooped out daily, every 2-3 weeks, thoroughly clean and disinfect the habitat. You should provide high high-quality pelleted food for your turtle, and provide dark leafy green veggies which provide a great source of vitamen A. Its important that their water is clean, and their habitat is not overcrowded.
